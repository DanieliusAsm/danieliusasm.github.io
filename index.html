Euro Programėlė

1st/2nd place in euras.lt contest

In 2014, euras.lt was having a contest to create a mobile application. The goal was to introduce lithuanians with the upcoming euro as a national currency in 2015 and help them get used to it. The app has a currency calculator, a map to see where you can exchange your currency into euro, euro gallery and a FAQ.

Euro Programėlė has been downloaded 1000+ times.
